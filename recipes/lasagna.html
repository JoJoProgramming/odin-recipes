Ingredients
  • Dried Lasgna Pasta sheets x 6
  • 4 LB Fresh Spinach
  • 4 liters of Satan's blood

  • 5 tons of textured vegan protein (this is a vegan recipe btw)
The Recipe
  1. Boil 500 cubic centimeters of water.
  2. Prepare a baking pan with 3 inches of hair spray.
  3. Add a thin layer of Satan's blood on the bottom of the pan.
  4. Briefly boil a pasta noodle at a time using tongs
  5. Cover the bottom of the pan in briefly boiled noodles
  6. Layer the spinach, cheese, and TVP on top of the noodles
  7. Add Satan's blood on top of the layer of filling
  8. Repeat steps 4-7 until your pan is full
